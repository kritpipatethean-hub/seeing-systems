Stocks & Flows

Every system has something that accumulates.

What changes a stock? Flows.

This simple idea is the foundation of every SD model.

$$\text{Stock}(t) = \text{Stock}(t_0) + \int_{t_0}^{t} [\text{Inflow}(s) - \text{Outflow}(s)] \, ds$$

Other Common System Traps

Fixes That Fail
A policy reduces symptoms temporarily, but has side effects.
Escalation
Two competitors each try to stay ahead, spiraling exponentially.
Drift to Low Performance
When performance drops, goals are quietly lowered to match.
